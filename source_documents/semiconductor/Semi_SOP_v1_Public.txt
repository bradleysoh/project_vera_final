Standard Operating Procedure: SOP-QA-101
Title: Wafer-Level Testing Procedure for RTX-Series Chips

1. Purpose
This SOP defines the standardized procedure for conducting wafer-level electrical tests on all RTX-series semiconductor chips prior to die singulation and packaging.

2. Scope
Applies to all RTX-series products including RTX-7000, RTX-8000, and RTX-9000.

3. Procedure
3.1 Set up the probe station per Equipment Manual EM-PS-200.
3.2 Apply voltage at rated maximum (refer to product datasheet for specific voltage limits).
3.3 Measure leakage current at each test point.
3.4 Record all measurements in the QA database.
3.5 Flag any die exceeding leakage threshold of 10uA as FAIL.

4. Acceptance Criteria
Yield must exceed 95% per wafer. Wafers below this threshold require MRB (Material Review Board) disposition.
