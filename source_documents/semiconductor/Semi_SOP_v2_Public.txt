Standard Operating Procedure: SOP-COMP-300
Title: Product Compliance Checklist - External Release

Before any RTX-series product is shipped to customers, the following compliance items must be verified:

1. Electrical specifications match the published datasheet.
2. All test results are within acceptance criteria.
3. Product labeling meets regulatory requirements (UL, CE, RoHS).
4. Material declarations (REACH, Conflict Minerals) are current.
5. Customer-specific requirements are documented and met.
6. Reliability test data (HTOL, TC, HAST) is on file.
7. All engineering changes have been formally approved via ECO.
8. No open CAPAs or NCRs that affect product quality.

Sign-off required from: QA Manager, Engineering Manager, and Compliance Officer before shipment release.
