Standard Operating Procedure: SOP-QA-205
Title: Internal Quality Audit Procedure
Classification: INTERNAL USE ONLY

1. Purpose
This SOP defines the procedure for conducting internal quality audits across all manufacturing and engineering departments.

2. Audit Schedule
Internal audits shall be conducted quarterly. Each department undergoes a minimum of two audits per year.

3. Audit Process
3.1 Select audit scope based on risk assessment matrix.
3.2 Review all process deviations and NCRs from the previous quarter.
3.3 Interview key personnel on process adherence.
3.4 Compare actual practices against documented SOPs.
3.5 Flag any undocumented process changes as non-conformances.
3.6 Issue audit report within 5 business days.

4. Escalation
Critical non-conformances must be escalated to the VP of Quality within 24 hours of discovery.
