Subject: URGENT - RTX-9000 Voltage Limit Revision
Date: 2024-11-15

Team,

Based on our latest thermal analysis and field failure data, we are IMMEDIATELY lowering the RTX-9000 maximum voltage limit from 5.0V to 3.3V. The 5.0V rating in the current datasheet (Rev 4.2) is no longer valid.

Root cause: Silicon lot #2024-Q3 shows accelerated electromigration at voltages above 3.5V, leading to premature failure. We've already seen 12 field returns linked to this issue.

Action items:
1. Update all test procedures to use 3.3V max
2. Notify customers with active orders
3. Datasheet revision to be published by EOW

This information is INTERNAL ONLY until the official datasheet revision is released.

- Dr. Sarah Chen, VP of Engineering
